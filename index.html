1.Press the any button to START Game.

2.Simon will give the first signal i.e. the block will be flash. Repeat the signal by pressing the same color blocks.

3.Simon will duplicate the first signal and add one. Repeat these two signals by pressing the same color blocks, in order.

4.Simon will duplicate these first two signals and add one.

5.Then level will be increasing.

6.Continue playing as long as you can repeat each sequence of signals correctly.

7.If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, Simon responds the whole screen will be red. This means you've lost, and the sequence of signals.

8.Lastly you got Game Over with until level.
